Popular Legal Procedures

How to transfer vehicle ownership

1. Obtain a notarized purchase agreement.
2. Verify there are no pending fines.
3. Submit documents to the Traffic Authority.
4. Pay transfer fees.
5. Finalize registration under the new owner.

How to request a cadastral certificate

1. Visit the Municipal Land Department website.
2. Complete the online application.
3. Attach property documents.
4. Pay applicable fees.
5. Download or receive the certificate by email.

IRS Certificate of No Outstanding Debts

1. Log in to the IRS online portal.
2. Go to the "Certificates" section.
3. Choose "No Outstanding Debts".
4. Generate and download the certificate.

How to register a lease contract

1. Fill in the municipal lease form.
2. Attach ID and property documents.
3. Pay the registration fee.
4. Receive the officially stamped copy.

How to request a criminal record certificate

1. Visit the Police Department website.
2. Log in using your ID.
3. Request the certificate.
4. Download the file or receive it by email.
